Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SudoDog Dashboard

What AI agent frameworks do you support?

SudoDog works with all major frameworks including LangChain, AutoGen, CrewAI, and custom implementations. Our SDK wraps your existing code with minimal changes.

How does the SDK integration work?

Install our Python SDK, add 3 lines of code, and your agents are immediately visible in the dashboard. We track all actions, LLM calls, and resource usage automatically.

Can I upgrade from Pro to Enterprise?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time. We'll prorate your billing and immediately unlock all Enterprise features including audit logs and Slack integration.

What security events do you detect?

We monitor for prompt injections, sensitive data exposure, anomalous behavior patterns, unauthorized access attempts, and resource abuse.

How accurate is cost tracking?

We track actual tokens used per LLM call and calculate costs based on provider pricing. Costs are updated in real-time and you can set budget alerts.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes! Annual billing gives you 2 months free (pay for 10, get 12). Contact us for custom enterprise contracts with SLAs.

What compliance standards do you meet?

Enterprise plan includes full audit logs for SOC2 Type II and GDPR compliance. Export logs as CSV for your compliance team or auditors.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan. No credit card required. Contact us for Enterprise trials.
